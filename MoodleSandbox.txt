Assign new function
1. Log in with "lcadmin"
2. Go to "Site administrator" > Server > External services
3. Under "Custom services" > "LC Web Services", click "Functions"
4. Click "Add functions" and select the required functions in the drop-down box
5. Click "Add functions" to update the system
